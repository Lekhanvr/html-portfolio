My Top Anime Recommendations

A Curated Selection of the Finest Anime

  1. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (2019)

    Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered. It is widely praised for its stunning animation, emotional storytelling, and intense battles.

  2. Vinland Saga (2019)

    Vinland Saga tells the story of Thorfinn, a young warrior caught between revenge and the harsh realities of war. It is a powerful tale of survival, loss, and the pursuit of true peace.

  3. Monster (2004)

    Monster follows Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a brilliant surgeon whose life changes after saving a boy who grows up to become a serial killer. It is a psychological thriller that deeply explores morality, justice, and the value of human life.
